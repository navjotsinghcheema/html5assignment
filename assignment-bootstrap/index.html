who we are

The Jeevan Hearts Foundation relies on the support of individuals, corporations and foundations to fund vital, life-saving research found in 2015. We have organized 12 blood donation camps in 5 cities so far and carried out 35 surgeries, saving the lives of 24. We strive to be fiscally responsible with your donations, with over 25% of CHF expenditures directly supporting research and research-related education.

who we are

Jeevan Hearts foundation was founded on 12 October 2014 by a group of college mates who wished to see a change in the society.

We started from the village near to our college, with statistics analysis to start with. Soon, we were joined by mbbs students who are still part of the organization. They are a bunch of really talented and dedicated individuals.

how we work

With a phenomenal team of doctors, We organize blood donation camps, undergo neccessory surgeries only dedicated to treat children born with congestial heart disease. The organization is a cummulative effort of many individuals with tremendous effort and passion. Individuals, volunteers and social enthusiasts work together for the better tommorow for some who could be the light of the dark world we live in.
