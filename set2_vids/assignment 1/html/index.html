who we are

The Jeevan Hearts Foundation relies on the support of individuals, corporations and foundations to fund vital, life-saving research found in 2015. We have organized 12 blood donation camps in 5 cities so far and carried out 35 surgeries, saving the lives of 24. We strive to be fiscally responsible with your donations, with over 25% of CHF expenditures directly supporting research and research-related education.

what is CHD?

Congenital Heart Disease(CHD) is a general term for the range of birth defects that affect the normal working of heart. CHD is one of the most common birth defects.

is it curable?

CHD is not curable. However, treatment can help manage the symptoms, furthur risk problems and prolong the life of an individual through medicine, surgery and adopting a certain lifestyle.

Statistics

About 78000 infants die every year in India because of CHD!
